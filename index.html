Tortoise and the animal kingdom

One day, the Tortoise decided that he wanted to throw a party. He invited all of his friends, including the Elephant, the Lion, and the Monkey.

The Tortoise told his friends that the party would be at his house, but he didn't actually have a house. So, on the day of the party, the Tortoise asked his friends to help him build a house.

The Elephant brought logs, the Lion brought stones, and the Monkey brought leaves. Together, they built a beautiful house for the Tortoise.

But when the party began, the Tortoise ate all of the food and drank all of the drinks himself, without sharing anything with his friends. Feeling angry and betrayed, the Elephant, Lion, and Monkey decided to teach the Tortoise a lesson. They told the Tortoise that they wanted to see him dance, and when he began to dance, they picked him up and threw him into a nearby river.
